ABOUT US

Problems are everywhere. Mankind understands some, fails to discern many. At Aufenbach, we are problem solvers. We don't believe in learning to live with the problems, but rather look for or come up with solutions. We intend to design products, solutions and frameworks to enable the human race to move forward. We cover various fields of science and technology at various scales, and strive to innovate and engineer solutions with the optimal balance of efficiency and quality for the given resources.
